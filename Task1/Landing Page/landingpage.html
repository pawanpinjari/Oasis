R.B Narayanrao Borawake College Shrirampur

Overview :-

           R.B. Narayanrao Borawake College is situated in Shrirampur in Maharashtra state of India. Established in 1960, RBNB is a Private college. The college is accredited by NAAC. R.B. Narayanrao Borawake College offers 22 courses across 3 streams namely Arts, Science, Management..Popular degrees offered at R.B. Narayanrao Borawake College include BSc, BA, MSc, MA, M.Phil..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, R.B.Narayanrao Borawake College is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond academics at R.B. Narayanrao Borawake College, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at R.B. Narayanrao Borawake College is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship
